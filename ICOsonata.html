Sonata.ai - The Only Crypto Exchange You will Ever Need

  • 08 March, 2019
  • ICO
  • Admin
  • 3

Abstract

About: Sonata is an all-in-one trading platform with long-term benefits for both operators and holders, created in cooperation with active exchange/Forex brokers and fintech enthusiasts.
Features: Security, friendly API, margin trading, and AI prediction.
Benefits: Cutting-edge hardware wallets, real-time market data, user-friendly interface, social interaction and chat rooms on the platform with brokers.
Tokenomics: Utility token. 1 SONT = 0.1 USD.
Bounty Dates: From November 1st 2018 to March 1st 2019.
Conclusion: We are talking about one of the most promising ICO projects of 2019, bringing us the most prominent features to keep in touch with blockchain technology.

Definition and goals

Sonata.ai is an all-in-one trading platform with long-term benefits for both operators and holders, created in cooperation with active exchange/Forex brokers and fintech enthusiasts.The most important advance is that the application will provide a solution for beginner and expert operators who want to focus on the business, instead of having to deal with a messy user interface and absurd functionality.Differentiating itself from the main exchange platforms, Sonata does not have to alter an already operative product and oblige users to acquire new usage patterns. Sonata.ai exchange does not need additional resources for the development itself. We are fully backed by a company in the financial sector.

Believing that personal participation is the key to building a healthy community, the main objective of Sonata.ai is to attract the first investors and share 80% of our income with them. In addition, the main vision is to create a balanced and easy-to-use product that is suitable for beginner users, while providing all the advanced features required by experienced investors.

Features

  • Perfect security
  • Built to perform
  • AI prediction
  • Margin trading
  • Social network elements
  • Friendly API

To secure all your investments on multiple levels user can focus on your business without worry.

Team designers have spent hours analyzing the user experience of professional brokers to build a truly distraction free interface.

Sonata is using real-time market data and an advanced AI algorithm to predict future price trends. It offers a great start for beginners.

Margin (leverage) trading is an expert tool used to level up the order income. The lender is rewarded with the interest.

Take a part in the buzz of cryptocurrency trading. Join rooms, send tips to other brokers or learn from the experts.

Sonata will provide a fully documented customizable API for your robots and other apps.

Advantages

  • All coins are securely stored on cutting-edge hardware wallets
  • Real-time market data
  • Advanced AI algorithm to predict future price trends
  • User-friendly fine-tuned interface for beginners and skilled brokers alike
  • Packed with advanced functionality
  • Full potential of chat rooms with expert brokers
